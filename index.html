Short Bio


I am a Lecturer (Research & Education) in Computer Science at University of Exeter, UK. I completed my EngD at University of Bath, UK and Plymouth Marine Laboratory, UK with a full studentship. I worked as a Postdoctoral Research Associate at University of Cambridge, UK and a Special Scientist at Technological University of Cyprus, Cyprus, where initiated and managed projects worth 400,000EUR. I co-supervised two PhD students; the joint paper with Dr Rorai Pereira Martins-Neto was selected at Editor's Choice Article in 2023 Series, Forests journal. At my current position, I have funding for a new PhD student. Also got the opportunity to be a Lead Guest Editor at the Remote Sensing journal. My work was included in the Most Notable Articles of the MDPI Remote Sensing Journal for December 2020 â€“ February 2021 and acknowledged as a distinguished contribution by Ladies of Landsat. My open source software (DASOS) was selected for inclusion in the Arctic code Vault Contributor of 2020 Github Archive Program. DASOS manages both discrete and full-waveform data, and it is fundamentally different from other software because it applies a rasterisation process first. My new tool, PlotToSat is also about to be published. PlotToSat simplifies the process of extracting time-series of Sentinel-1 and Sentinel-2 data at multiple to thousands of plot regions. Experience in both academia and industrial innovation was achieved, through my international placements that include Planetek Italia, Italy, and Interpine Group Ltd, New Zealand.
Currently seeking new collaborations for joint proposal writing. For information about my work, please have a look at my projects and publications.
